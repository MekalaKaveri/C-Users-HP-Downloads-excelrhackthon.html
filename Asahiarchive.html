About Us

The Asahi Archives is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich tapestry of Indian civilization and culture. We embark on a collective journey to unearth the past, celebrate the present, and unravel the mysteries of the Indian subcontinent.

Unveiling India's Legacy:A Call to Discover Unearth the Past: Our mission is to meticulously document India's history, from the Indus Valley Civilization to the Mughal era and beyond. We conduct research projects, partner with archaeological expeditions, and collect historical artifacts and records.

Celebrate the Present: India's vibrant culture thrives in its languages, art forms, folk traditions, and diverse communities. We document these living expressions through multimedia archives, festival celebrations, and educational workshops.

Unravel the Mysteries: The Indian subcontinent is a land of ancient lore and enigmatic sites. We investigate mysterious places, folk legends, and unexplained phenomena, employing scientific inquiry alongside indigenous knowledge.

Why is this important? Preserving our heritage:India's legacy is a precious resource, shaping our identity and inspiring future generations.

Fostering cultural understanding:By documenting our diverse traditions, we promote tolerance and appreciation for India's multifaceted nature.

curiosity: We unveil the captivating stories behind our history, sparking a passion for exploration and discovery.
